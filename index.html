Dataset for "Exploring Viewer Gazing Patterns for Touch-based Mobile Gamecasting "

NOTE: The datasets presented below are for academic use only.

Introduction

To collect user's touch data, we install the Android Debug Bridge (ADB) on a desktop PC (DELL Optiplex 7010) that connects to a mobile phone (Samsung Galaxy S5 with Android 6.0.1). To collect user's gazing data, we choose Tobii eyeX as the eye-tracking device to collect the users' gazing data due to its affordable price, suitable sampling rate, and high accuracy. It is connected to a desktop PC (DELL Optiplex 7010) through USB 3.0 port and attached to the frame of a 27-inch monitor (DELL U2715H). Currently, we only provide samples, if you need full dataset, please send email to us.
